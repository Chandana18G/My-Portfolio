About Me

Who I am.

Hey! I'm Chandana, a Master's student in Applied Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at SRH University Munich. Originally from Mysuru, India — now building my life and career in Germany.

I work with machine learning, data analytics, and Python to solve real-world problems. I've interned as a Data Science Intern at TechnoTut and a Data Analyst Trainee at SPORTS-KPI, building everything from ML classification models to Tableau KPI dashboards.
