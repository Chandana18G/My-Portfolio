2019 The Unconventional Start

2019 marked the start of everything. I began my Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering at JSS Polytechnic for Women in Mysuru, India.

After completing my 10th grade, I faced the common dilemma of choosing between science streams. Instead of following the traditional route, I chose a Diploma in Computer Science to gain practical knowledge and hands-on experience. This decision marked the beginning of my journey into technology.During my diploma, I actively participated in technical competitions and extracurricular activities, including a fashion show. These experiences helped me build confidence, explore my interests, and grow beyond academics.In my final year, I appeared for the engineering entrance exam and secured a rank of 211 among 40,000 candidates. This achievement reinforced my confidence and opened the door to pursue a degree in Computer Science.
